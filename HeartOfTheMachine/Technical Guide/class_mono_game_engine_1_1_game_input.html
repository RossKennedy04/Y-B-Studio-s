Parameters

rightVibrationThe strength with which to vibrate the gamepad's right motor. Should be between 0.0f and 1.0f.
duration[Optional] The amount of time, in seconds, that the vibration should last for. '0.1f' by default.
player[Optional] The index of the desired gamepad. Should be between 1 and 8. '1' by default.
